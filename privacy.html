PRIVACY

Privacy

This storefront lets you browse the collection, keep items in a shopping bag, and save an order request in your browser without payment.

Information you choose to share

If you contact us through Instagram, you choose which product, fit, country, and contact details to include in your message.

How it is used

Information you send is used to answer your question and provide product, fit, or customer-care support.

Order requests

An order request stores only the selected product, quantity, optional country or region, optional wrist size, and request reference in your browser. It does not collect a name, email address, phone number, or delivery address.

Third-party services

With your consent, Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technology to collect aggregated visit and interaction data. Advertising storage and personalization remain disabled. Instagram processes messages under its own privacy terms.

Analytics choice

You can accept or decline analytics on the storefront. We do not send names, email addresses, postal addresses, wrist measurements, or message content to Google Analytics. Learn how Google processes service data at Google's partner sites policy.

Correction and deletion

You may ask us through Instagram to correct or delete information you sent to customer care. We do not sell contact information.
