SHIPPING

Shipping

You may submit an order request without payment. An order request does not reserve stock or arrange shipping.

Availability

Adding an item to your shopping bag or recording an order request does not create a paid or confirmed order.

Delivery estimates

Available destinations, shipping charges, and order-specific delivery estimates will be shown before an order can be placed.

Tracking

Tracking details and the carrier will be provided for eligible confirmed orders after dispatch.

Delivery issues

Customer care will provide the applicable options for a confirmed order. Please include the order number when order support becomes available.
